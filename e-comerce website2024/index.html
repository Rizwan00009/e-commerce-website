Reinhold Messner

September 17, 1944

Reinhold Messner, born on September 17, 1944, in the Italian province of South Tyrol, is renowned as one of the greatest mountaineers of all time. His remarkable career groundbreaking ascents, including being the first person to climb all fourteen peaks over 8,000 meters (known as the Eight-thousanders) without supplemental oxygen.

Walter Bonatti

June 22, 1930

Walter Bonatti, born on June 22, 1930, in Bergamo, Italy, was a celebrated Italian mountaineer known for his daring ascents and contributions to alpinism. He gained ascent of a new route on the south-west pillar of the Dru in the French Alps in 1955, a climb that pushed the boundaries of what was thought possible at the time.

Alex Honnold

August 17, 1985

Alex Honnold, born on August 17, 1985, in Sacramento, California, is a renowned American rock climber known for his extraordinary feats of free solo climbing.
